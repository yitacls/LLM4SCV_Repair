Although these functions are restricted with onlyOwner, they present an unprotected/unsafe withdrawal pattern:\n\n- withdrawCrypto uses a low-level call to transfer the entire contract balance to an arbitrary beneficiary address provided by the caller. Using call with the full balance and emitting the balance after transfer (which will be zero) is error-prone. If the beneficiary is a contract, fallback logic could be triggered during the transfer; while onlyOwner restricts who can initiate the call, a malicious or compromised owner account (or owner-controlled contract) can drain funds unexpectedly. Also, the function lacks a reentrancy guard and uses address(this).balance in both the call and the emitted event, which can produce misleading logs.\n\n- withdrawTokens transfers the entire balance of any specified token to the beneficiary.
