If duration and rentPrice are large enough their product will wrap modulo 2^256, producing an incorrect (typically much smaller) price. An attacker or caller could request a very large duration such that the multiplication overflows, causing the returned price to be drastically reduced and enabling underpayment relative to the intended cost.
