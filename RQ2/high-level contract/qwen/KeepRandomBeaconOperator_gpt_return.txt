Looking at the contract, I can identify the SWC-120 vulnerability in the `getDelayFactor()` function at line 471, where `block.number` is used as a source of randomness. This is problematic because miners can manipulate block numbers within a small range.

The vulnerability occurs in the delay factor calculation where `block.number` is used to determine submission timing, which can be manipulated by miners.
