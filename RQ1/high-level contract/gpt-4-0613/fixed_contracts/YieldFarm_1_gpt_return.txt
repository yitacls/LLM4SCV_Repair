In the above code, the DoS with Block Gas Limit vulnerability is fixed by storing intermediate results in a dynamic array instead of multiple transfer calls. SafeMath library is not needed anymore because overflow and underflow protection are built-in in Solidity version 0.8.0 and above. To note, this change does not change the contract's original functionality.
