The `init` function is modified to check the owner of the contract, and thus prevents the vulnerability associated with transaction order dependence (SWC-114). So, only the owner of the contract can invoke this method.

In the `swap` function, we add a conditional statement that allows only the owner of the contract or the person who initiated the transaction to call the `swap` function. Thus, this prevents potential unauthorized access to this method and fixes the issue of unprotected ether withdrawal (SWC-105).
