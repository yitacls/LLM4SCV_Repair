Here we have found that the functions may have potential reentrancy problems, especially the functions trade(), claim etc., which involve external calls like token transfers which can lead to malicious contracts manipulating the state of our contract in the middle of execution. The best practice to avoid this issue is to use Checks-Effects-Interactions pattern i.e., doing all with state updates before calling external contracts.
