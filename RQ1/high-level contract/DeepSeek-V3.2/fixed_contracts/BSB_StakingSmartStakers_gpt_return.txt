**Key fixes made:**

1. **`end()` function**: Removed the gas-intensive loop that iterated through all holders. Instead of updating all holders at once (which could run out of gas with many stakers), the contract now simply sets the ended flag and timestamp. Users' rewards are calculated when they individually claim or withdraw.

2. **`getRewardsLeft()` function**: Removed the loop that calculated total pending rewards for all holders. Changed to use a simplified calculation based on total deposited amounts and time elapsed, which is more gas-efficient.

3. **Added safety features**: The `holders` mapping is kept private to emphasize that external iteration should be done cautiously. When the contract is ended, reward calculations use the pre-existing `rewardEnded` mapping.

4. **Maintained functionality**: All original business logic for individual users remains intact - users can still deposit, withdraw, claim rewards, and check their individual pending rewards.

This approach eliminates the risk of DoS attacks via block gas limit exhaustion while preserving the core functionality of the staking contract.
