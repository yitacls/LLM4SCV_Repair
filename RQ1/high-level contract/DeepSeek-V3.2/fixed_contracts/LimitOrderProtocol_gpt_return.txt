Looking at the contract, I can identify two main vulnerabilities: reentrancy (SWC-107) and potential DoS with block gas limit (SWC-128). Let me analyze and fix them:

1. **Reentrancy Vulnerability**: In `fillOrderTo`, there's a call to `InteractiveMaker.notifyFillOrder()` before transferring maker assets to taker. This could allow reentrancy attacks.

2. **DoS with Block Gas Limit**: The `notifyFillOrder` call could potentially consume excessive gas, causing transaction failures.
