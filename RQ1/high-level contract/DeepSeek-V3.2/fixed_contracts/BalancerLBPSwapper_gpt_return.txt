Let's analyze the vulnerabilities step by step:

1. **SWC-114 Transaction Order Dependence**: In the `init()` function, there's no access control check - anyone can initialize the contract. This should be restricted.

2. **SWC-105 Unprotected Ether Withdrawal**: In the `swap()` function, the transfer `IERC20(tokenReceived).transfer(...)` happens without any access control, allowing anyone to trigger it. Also, there's no validation to prevent multiple concurrent swaps or ensure proper auction state.
